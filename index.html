Frequently Asked Questions

We buy all types of gold including jewelry, coins, bars, broken ornaments, and old gold items. We accept gold from 8K to 24K purity levels.

We use advanced XRF (X-Ray Fluorescence) machines that provide accurate purity testing without damaging your gold. The entire process is done in front of you for complete transparency.

You need to bring a valid government-issued photo ID (Aadhar Card, PAN Card, Driving License, or Passport) and the original purchase invoice of the gold if available.

Our gold prices are based on current market rates, purity level, and weight. We provide the best competitive rates with no hidden charges or unnecessary deductions.

We offer cash payment up to ₹10,000. For amounts above ₹10,000, we provide instant bank transfer through NEFT, IMPS, or RTGS directly to your account.
