Our Service Process

1

Initial Assessment

Bring your gold items to our branch or schedule a mobile van visit. Our experts will conduct an initial visual assessment of your gold.

2

Ultrasonic Cleaning

Your gold items are cleaned using our ultrasonic machines to remove all dirt and impurities, ensuring accurate weight measurement.

3

Purity Testing

Advanced XRF machine testing determines the exact purity of your gold. This non-destructive process is performed in front of you.

4

Weight & Valuation

Your gold is weighed on certified digital scales, and the value is calculated based on current market rates and purity levels.

5

Final Offer

We present you with a transparent, fair offer based on the assessment. All calculations are shown clearly with no hidden charges.

6

Instant Payment

Upon acceptance, receive immediate cash payment or instant bank transfer. All documentation is provided for your records.
