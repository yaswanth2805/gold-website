What We Do

Our comprehensive services in gold buying.

Our Services

At SRISTAR GOLD COMPANY, our primary service is the buying of old and used gold in various forms, including jewelry, coins, and bars. We ensure a professional and secure process from start to finish.

Our process involves:

  • Transparent Valuation: We use advanced XRF machines to accurately assess the purity of your gold, right in front of you.
  • Instant Payment: Once the valuation is complete and you agree to the price, we offer immediate payment through your preferred method (cash, bank transfer, etc.).
  • Expert Consultation: Our knowledgeable staff are available to answer all your questions and provide detailed explanations of our procedures.
  • Secure Environment: All transactions are conducted in a safe and comfortable environment, ensuring your peace of mind.

We also offer ultrasonic cleaning for your gold items to prepare them for accurate assessment, free of charge. Our commitment is to make selling your gold a simple, trustworthy, and rewarding experience.
