We use advanced XRF (X-Ray Fluorescence) machines that provide accurate purity testing without damaging your gold. The entire process is done in front of you for complete transparency. Our equipment is calibrated regularly to ensure precise readings.

Our evaluation process typically takes 15-30 minutes depending on the number of items. We carefully examine each piece, test purity, weigh accurately, and provide detailed explanations of our valuation process.

Technical Questions

24K gold is pure gold (99.9% purity), while 22K gold contains 91.6% gold mixed with other metals for durability. 24K gold is softer and more valuable per gram, while 22K gold is commonly used in jewelry making due to its strength and durability.

Gold-plated jewelry has very minimal gold content and is not suitable for gold buying. We only purchase solid gold items. Our testing equipment can easily distinguish between solid gold and gold-plated items.

We can buy gold jewelry with gemstones, but we only pay for the gold content. Gemstones are carefully removed and returned to you, or you can remove them beforehand. The gold weight is calculated after removing all non-gold components.

We use certified digital scales that are accurate to 0.01 grams and are calibrated regularly. Our scales are verified by authorized agencies and we're happy to demonstrate their accuracy with standard weights.
